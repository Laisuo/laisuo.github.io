Material Characterization Technique - XRR

Posted on 2018-10-04 | In Experiment , Research |

Introduction

X-ray reflectivity (XRR) is a non-destructive method and works for crystalline and amorphous materials. It can measure film thickness from 0.1 nm - 1000 nm. It is used to analyze X-ray reflection intensity curves from grazing incident X-ray beam to determine thin-film parameters including thickness, density, and surface or interface roughness.
